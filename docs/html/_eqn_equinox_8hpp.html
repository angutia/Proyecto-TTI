Detailed Description

This header contains the EqnEquinox operation. It computes the equation of the equinoxes. The equation of the equinoxes dpsi*cos(eps) is the right ascension of the mean equinox referred to the true equator and equinox and is equal to the difference between apparent and mean sidereal time.
